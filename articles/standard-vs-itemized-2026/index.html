Standard vs. Itemized Deductions in 2026: Which Saves You More?

Standard vs. Itemized Deductions in 2026: Which Saves You More?

What Counts as an Itemized Deduction?

Itemizing means listing each tax-deductible expense individually and subtracting the total from your adjusted gross income (AGI). The most common itemized deductions in 2026 are:

  • State and local taxes (SALT): state income (or sales) tax plus property tax, with the cap raised from $10,000 to $40,000 for 2026.
  • Mortgage interest: interest on up to $750,000 of home acquisition debt.
  • Charitable contributions: cash and non-cash gifts with proper receipts.
  • Medical expenses: out-of-pocket medical and dental costs above 7.5% of AGI.
  • Disaster losses: uninsured property losses in federally declared disaster areas.
The Break-Even Test
Filing Status2026 Standard Deduction
Single$16,100
Married Filing Jointly$32,200
Married Filing Separately$16,100
Head of Household$24,150

The rule is simple: if your itemized total is below the standard deduction for your filing status, take the standard deduction; if it is above, itemize. Example: a married couple paying $18,000 in state and property taxes, $12,000 in mortgage interest, and $3,000 in charitable gifts totals $33,000 — barely above the $32,200 standard deduction. Itemizing wins, but by only $800, so weigh the recordkeeping burden.

Who Should Itemize in 2026?

With the SALT cap now at $40,000, homeowners in high-tax states such as California, New York, and New Jersey benefit the most: $25,000 of state tax plus $12,000 of mortgage interest plus $4,000 of donations is $41,000, well above the joint standard deduction. Renters in low-tax states, by contrast, usually still come out ahead with the standard deduction.

什么是逐项扣除 (Itemized Deductions)？

逐项扣除是把税法允许的各项支出逐一列出，从调整后总收入 (AGI) 中扣除。2026 年常见的逐项扣除项目包括：

  • 州和地方税 (SALT)：州所得税（或销售税）加房产税，2026 年上限从 $10,000 提高到 $40,000。
  • 房贷利息：最多 $750,000 购房贷款所产生的利息。
  • 慈善捐赠：现金及实物捐赠（需保留凭证）。
  • 医疗费用：超过 AGI 7.5% 的自付医疗和牙科费用。
  • 灾害损失：联邦宣布灾区内未获保险赔付的财产损失。

规则很简单：逐项扣除总额低于标准扣除额，就选标准扣除；高于它，就逐项扣除。举例：一对 MFJ 夫妻缴纳 $18,000 州税与房产税、$12,000 房贷利息、$3,000 慈善捐赠，合计 $33,000，略高于 $32,200 的标准扣除——逐项扣除胜出，但仅多 $800，是否值得保留全年凭证要自行权衡。

2026 年谁最该逐项扣除？

SALT 上限提高到 $40,000 后，高税州（如加州、纽约、新泽西）的房主受益最大：$25,000 州税加 $12,000 房贷利息加 $4,000 捐赠就是 $41,000，明显超过 MFJ 标准扣除。相反，无房贷、居住在低税州的家庭通常仍选标准扣除。
