Long-Term Capital Gains Tax: The 0%, 15%, and 20% Brackets Explained

Sell an appreciated asset — stocks, funds, real estate — and capital gains tax comes into play. How long you held the asset changes everything: short-term gains are taxed as ordinary income at up to 37%, while long-term gains enjoy preferential rates of 0%, 15%, or 20%.

Short-Term vs. Long-Term: One Year Is the Line

Assets held one year or less produce short-term gains, folded into ordinary income and taxed at the regular 10%–37% brackets. Assets held longer than one year — at least a year and a day — qualify as long-term and use the special lower rates.

How Gains Stack on Top of Ordinary Income

To find your long-term rate, first tally your ordinary taxable income, then stack the gains on top. Example: a Single filer with $40,000 of ordinary taxable income and a $20,000 long-term gain — the 0% bracket runs to $49,450, leaving $9,450 of room, so $9,450 of the gain is tax-free and the remaining $10,550 is taxed at 15%.

Watch for the NIIT

High-income investors may also owe the 3.8% Net Investment Income Tax when MAGI exceeds $200,000 (Single) or $250,000 (MFJ). This calculator does not model the NIIT, so add it yourself if you are above those levels.

Tax-Loss Harvesting and the Wash-Sale Rule

Selling losers offsets gains, and up to $3,000 of net loss can offset ordinary income each year, with the rest carried forward. Beware the wash-sale rule: buying a "substantially identical" security within 30 days before or after the sale disallows the loss.

💡 Planning to sell a winning position? Holding it for at least a year and a day converts a short-term gain into a long-term one — that single move can cut the tax rate on the profit from 37% to 15%, or even 0%.

卖出升值的资产——股票、基金、房产——就要面对资本利得税。持有时间长短决定了税率天差地别：短期按普通收入最高 37% 征税，长期则享受 0%、15%、20% 三档优惠税率。

短期 vs 长期：一年是关键

持有不超过一年即卖出的资产产生短期资本利得，并入普通收入按 10%–37% 的常规税率征税。持有超过一年（至少一年零一天）则属长期资本利得，适用专门的低税率。

利得如何「叠」在普通收入之上

确定长期利得税率时，先算普通应税收入，再把利得叠在上面。例：单身纳税人普通应税收入 $40,000，另有 $20,000 长期利得——0% 档到 $49,450 为止还有 $9,450 空间，因此 $9,450 的利得免税，其余 $10,550 按 15% 征税。

注意 NIIT 附加税

高收入投资者还可能面临 3.8% 的净投资收入税 (Net Investment Income Tax)：MAGI 超过 $200,000（单身）/ $250,000（MFJ）时对投资收入触发。本计算器未建模 NIIT，高收入者请自行加计。

税损收割 (Tax-Loss Harvesting) 与洗售规则

卖出亏损资产可以抵消利得；净亏损中最多 $3,000 可抵扣普通收入，余额结转以后年度。但要小心洗售规则 (Wash-Sale Rule)：卖出亏损资产前后 30 天内买回「实质相同」的证券，亏损将被驳回。

💡 计划卖出盈利仓位时，尽量持有满一年零一天再卖，把短期利得变成长期利得——仅这一个动作就可能把该笔收益的税率从 37% 降到 15% 甚至 0%。
