What's New for 2026: How the One Big Beautiful Bill Act Affects Your Taxes

What's New for 2026: How the One Big Beautiful Bill Act Affects Your Taxes

The Biggest Changes for 2026

The One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA), signed into law in 2025, reshaped the 2026 tax year — the first full filing season under the new rules. The most far-reaching change is a larger standard deduction: $16,100 for Single and Married Filing Separately, $32,200 for Married Filing Jointly, and $24,150 for Head of Household. Because roughly nine out of ten filers claim the standard deduction, this one change automatically lowers taxable income for most households.

A Bigger Child Tax Credit

For 2026, the Child Tax Credit rises from $2,000 to $2,200 per qualifying child under 17, and up to $1,700 of that amount is refundable through the Additional Child Tax Credit. The income phase-outs still begin at $200,000 for single filers and $400,000 for joint filers, with the credit shrinking gradually above those levels.

Brand-New Deductions
  • Senior deduction: taxpayers age 65 and older can claim an additional $6,000 deduction (income limits apply).
  • Tips deduction: up to $25,000 of qualified tip income may be deductible.
  • Overtime deduction: up to $12,500 of qualified overtime pay may be deductible.
  • Car-loan interest: up to $10,000 of interest on a loan for a new, US-assembled vehicle may be deductible.
  • SALT relief: the state and local tax deduction cap rises from $10,000 to $40,000 for 2026, making itemizing worthwhile again for many households in high-tax states.

2026 年最重要的变化一览

《One Big Beautiful Bill Act》（简称 OBBBA）于 2025 年签署成为法律，重塑了 2026 税年的格局，2026 也是首个完整适用新规的报税季。影响最广的变化是标准扣除额全面提高：单身（Single）与已婚分别申报（MFS）为 $16,100，已婚联合申报（MFJ）为 $32,200，户主（Head of Household）为 $24,150。由于约九成纳税人使用标准扣除，仅这一项变化就自动降低了大多数家庭的应税收入。

儿童税收抵免 (CTC) 上调

2026 年，每位 17 岁以下符合条件儿童的 CTC 从 $2,000 提高到 $2,200，其中最多 $1,700 可通过 Additional Child Tax Credit 退还（即可退税部分）。收入逐步取消门槛保持不变：单身 $200,000、已婚联合 $400,000 起开始递减。

全新的扣除项目
  • 老年人扣除：65 岁及以上纳税人可额外享受 $6,000 扣除（有收入限制）。
  • 小费扣除：最多 $25,000 的合格小费收入可扣除。
  • 加班费扣除：最多 $12,500 的合格加班费可扣除。
  • 车贷利息扣除：购买美国组装新车的贷款利息最多可扣除 $10,000。
  • SALT 上限提高：州和地方税扣除上限从 $10,000 提高到 $40,000，让高税州的许多家庭重新值得逐项扣除。
本计算器覆盖哪些内容

本计算器完整建模了 2026 年税率表、标准扣除、CTC、FICA（社保与医保税）以及长期资本利得税，但尚未建模小费、加班费、老年人和车贷利息等新扣除。如果您符合这些新条款，实际税额可能低于本工具的估算结果。完整计算流程见「计算详解」标签页。

⚠️ OBBBA 新条款附带资格规则、收入逐步取消和凭证要求，IRS 仍在陆续发布实施细则。在申报小费、加班费、老年人或车贷利息扣除前，请咨询专业税务人士或查阅最新的 IRS 官方出版物。
